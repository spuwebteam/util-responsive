Where does our water come from?

Seattle's water comes from two regional mountain watersheds. We protect our watersheds and monitor our supply to give you fresh, safe drinking water.

Protect your pipes from freezing

When the temperature drops below freezing, the water pipes in your home can freeze and break. Find out how to protect them.

Compost Christmas trees for free

From Dec. 26 through Jan. 31, Seattle residents can compost holiday trees, wreathes, and greens for free. Make sure to remove any metal, flocking (artificial snow), plastic, or ornaments before setting out for your weekly collection.
